Banh mi chao (Bread with combo pan) is a Vietnamese-styled hot alternative to stuffed baguettes. Bread is served in a frying pan with eggs, pate and sausage. But this is just the basic version. A wide variety of "fillings" are available for hungry diners to choose from.
